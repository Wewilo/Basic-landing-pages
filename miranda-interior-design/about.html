About Miranda

Hi there! I'm Miranda Jay, an interior designer based in Nashville. I believe that everyone deserves a beautiful and functional space that reflects their unique style.

I'm passionate about design and love working with clients to create spaces they love. I'm known for my ability to really listen and understand my clients' needs and desires, whether it's a full home redesign or just refreshing a single room.

When I'm not designing, you can usually find me exploring the local farmers' market or trying out a new recipe in my kitchen. I love incorporating fresh ingredients and bold flavors into my cooking, just like I love incorporating unique textures and colors into my design work.

Let's work together to make your design dreams a reality!
